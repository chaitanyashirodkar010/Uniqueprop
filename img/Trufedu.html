what we do

Here are four brief reasons why everyone needs trufedu in their life.

It's all about helping YOU.

Trufedu is a customer-focused company; we are there for you for all your needs. Our team will guide you, help you figure things out and support you throughout till your needs and goals will not be achieved.

We help and educate you.

Our mission is not just to help you but also teach you what's best for your needs. May it be your grandfather's will or planning for your child's marriage. We will help you find a solution tailored to your needs and budget.

Experts for every need

Just like a puzzle, not every expert will be a match for you, so we do all the headache and assign you a professionally certified expert having expertise in your current requirement/ issue.

24x7 Assistance

Our excellent and professional team of support is always there for you 365 days a year. May it be an Independence day or even Diwali, if you are our client we are there for you.

about us
something about the company

Trufedu in Year 2019, in the heart of India’s Delhi. Prime aim behind establishing Trufedu has been to educate people about financial Planning. Trufedu has a wide variety of service for all scales of people, which include Legal help, Trip consultation, Life Insurances, Loans & many more. These financial services were initially provisioned to HNI and UHNI clients only, but in Trufedu we aim to entrust this VIP level service for everyone, at a very affordable cost.
